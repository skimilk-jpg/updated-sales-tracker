Sales Prediction Engine

Once enough Square data is loaded, the engine switches to a data-calibrated model. All factors below are derived from your actual sales history.

FactorHow it works
BaseYour overall average daily net sales across all historical data.
Day of WeekEach weekday gets a multiplier from its actual average vs the overall base. E.g. if Saturdays average 20% above base, Saturday predictions are scaled up 20%.
SeasonalityEach calendar month gets a multiplier from its actual monthly average. Shown only when the month impact is > $30 above/below base.
TemperatureBins: <−10°C, −10–0°C, 0–5°C, 5–12°C, 12–18°C, 18–26°C, >26°C. Multiplier for each bin is calibrated from days in that temperature range vs the base.
Weather ConditionWMO code bins: Clear / Partly Cloudy / Overcast / Drizzle / Rain / Snow. Each bin has a data-derived multiplier. Temperature and condition are averaged together.
Home GamesRaptors, Maple Leafs, and Blue Jays home games. Lift % is calibrated from your actual sales on game days vs comparable non-game days.
Other EventsManually added events (concerts, festivals, etc.) contribute a flat ~$80 lift per event unless tagged as a closure.
ClosureEvents tagged as closures set predicted sales to ~10% of base.
Confidence RangeLow = −10% of prediction · High = +12% of prediction.
